Anatomy Study Guide

What percent of your body's blood is in your kidneys at any point in time?

20%.

What is your upper arm bone called?

It is called your humerus.

How many sacral vertebrae do you have?

You may have 4 or 5, depending on if certain vertebrae have fused or not.

What percent of the human body is water?

55-60% of the human body is water.

Where does internal respiration happen?

Internal respiration happens in the capillary walls, in every cell of the human body.

What muscle is often called the calf muscle?

The anatomically correct name for the calf muscle is the gastrocnemius.

Which portion of your brain controls and regulates the endocrine system?

The hypothalamus.

Is the ovary attached to the fallopian tube?

No.
